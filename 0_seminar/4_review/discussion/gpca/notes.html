pca - incomplete data case

⚡ incomplete data, especially nmar

  • standard pca not applicable to data with missing values
  • standard imputation techniques available, but
    • ⚡ mean imputation reduces overall variance
    • ⚡ imputation by regression might distort marginal/joint distributions
    • ⚡ additional uncertainty of imputation is not accounted for
